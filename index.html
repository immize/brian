About Me

BUILT ON
RELATIONSHIPS.
DRIVEN BY
PURPOSE.

"If simplifying your message feels difficult, you're not alone."

For the past 15 years, I've helped organizations distill their story and create content with heart — content that moves their audience to action. I've been fortunate to build relationships with incredibly talented people in the film industry, meaning project crews and resources can scale to fit your goals and budget.

From two-man documentary crews in Hamburg to nine-person brand productions across Texas, I adapt to what the story needs. The mission stays the same: clear message, emotional connection, something personal.

How We
Work Together

01

Story Discovery

We find the emotional core of your message — the thing your audience will actually feel. No brief goes untouched until it's clear and real.

02

Creative Development

Concept, script, and pitch. I develop the creative direction and present it to your team before a single light is set up.

03

Crew & Pre-Production

I assemble the right crew for your scale and budget — from a lean two-person team to a full nine-person production, vetting every hire myself.

04

Production

On-set leadership, schedule management, and the flexibility to follow the story wherever it leads — including internationally.

05

Post-Production

Edit, color, sound, and revision feedback to make sure the final film matches the original vision — not just the latest request.

06

Distribution Strategy

A great film needs the right home. I help think through how your story lands — events, social, web, internal — so it does the job it was made for.
